Abstract

This study classified tweets posted by four corporate twitter accounts according to the contents in order to analyze mar-keting strategies. Moreover, key findings include usage types of corporation and followed key players. further findings were made by investigating four more corporate accounts for the comparison between same line of business. Usage type was classified as six types: daily life, general publicity, event, professional information, customize and retweet. We aimed to find key players with influence formula and give a clear sense by visualization. The study reveals that most corporation apply cus-tomize types for customer communication, and followers show the most active reaction on event type of tweets. It is thus essential to adopt customized strate-gies such as building familiar relationship with key players, or maintain-ing/reinforcing the influence of key players. This research is expected to pro-vi-de valuable insights for corporation to apply effective and specific social me-dia marketing strategies.
